MERN E-commerce Mobile App

The project is a mobile e-commerce application. It offers two user profiles: normal users and administrators. Normal users can browse products, choose between cash on delivery or online payment via Stripe, and track their orders. Administrators have a dashboard to manage the product catalog, monitor orders, and visualize inventory using a pie chart. This application aims to provide an easy and convenient shopping experience for users and efficient management tools for administrators.
